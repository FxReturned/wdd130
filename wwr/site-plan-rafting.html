Overview

Purpose

Online presence aims to drive bookings, educate about rafting, boost brand awareness, engage visitors, and offer customer support. Our site will showcase thrilling experiences to attract and inform potential customers effectively.

Audience

Our target audience for the water rafting website includes adventure enthusiasts aged 18-40, with moderate to high disposable income and a passion for outdoor activities. They seek thrilling experiences, safety information, booking convenience, and engaging content. They primarily access our site via mobile devices and desktops.
